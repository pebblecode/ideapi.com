Welcome to ideapi

ideapi is a brand new tool to help groups of people work together better, giving them the ability to create and agree a clear brief and make live updates.

An excellent application for busy diaries, remote working and collaboration.

Currently in private BETA, ideapi is scheduled for public release in early February 2010.

ideapi is currently in use by: creative agencies, copy writters, script writters and teachers.
